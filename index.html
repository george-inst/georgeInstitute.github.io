About GPS

Welcome to the George Educational Institute This institute was established keeping in view national as well as Defence requirements and motivated by the keen interest generated by the young Turks right from the tender age of ten years. This is the first institute of its kind in India which has fully fledged Network and Faculty to coach boys & girls appearing for the various entrance exams of UPSC to Defence-oriented establishments such as Rashtriya India Military College (R.I.M.C.) Military school/sainik school, National Defence Academy (NDA),Combined Defence Service Examinations (CDSE), Air Force, Navy, A.C.C & Women Ent.

Infrastructure

George Public School is equipped with 19 rooms, labs to conduct practicals in Physics, Chemistry, Computer Science. All the labs are set up with latest technology and have all necessary equipment and requirements. The labs are spacious and have proper sitting arrangements.

We are heading towards smart learning techniques.All classes are having SMART BOARDS to help students learn more.We have separate SPORTS GROUND to conduct various sports activities and a BASKETBALL court inside the Campus. BUS Facility is also provided to the students .
